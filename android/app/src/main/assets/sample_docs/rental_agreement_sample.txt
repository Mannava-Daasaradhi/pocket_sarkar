RESIDENTIAL RENTAL AGREEMENT

This Rental Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on this 1st day of April 2025
between the Landlord, Mr. Ramesh Sharma, residing at 14-B, Sainik Colony, Hyderabad
("Landlord"), and the Tenant, Ms. Priya Patel ("Tenant").

PROPERTY: 3BHK Apartment at Flat 402, Lakshmi Towers, Banjara Hills, Hyderabad 500034.

RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS
1. Monthly Rent: Rs. 35,000/- per month payable by the 5th of each month.
2. Security Deposit: Rs. 2,10,000/- (Six months rent) payable at execution of this Agreement.
3. AUTO-DEBIT MANDATE: The Tenant hereby irrevocably authorizes the Landlord to initiate
   an Electronic Clearing Service (ECS) / NACH auto-debit instruction from the Tenant's
   bank account for the monthly rent and any outstanding dues without requiring prior
   written notice to the Tenant. The Tenant shall maintain sufficient balance.
4. Late Payment Penalty: If rent is not credited by the 5th of the month, a penalty of
   Rs. 500/- per day will be charged. In the event of default exceeding 15 days, a
   compounding penalty of 36% per annum shall be applied on all outstanding amounts
   including previously accrued penalties.

SECURITY DEPOSIT REFUND
5. The Security Deposit or any portion thereof shall be refunded to the Tenant within
   90 days of vacating the premises at the sole and absolute discretion of the Landlord.
   The Landlord shall be the sole and final arbiter of what constitutes damage beyond
   normal wear and tear. No appeal or dispute mechanism is available for the Tenant.
6. The Landlord may deduct from the deposit: painting charges (full apartment, regardless
   of tenure), professional cleaning charges, "opportunity cost" for time taken to re-let
   the property, and any other amounts the Landlord in his sole discretion deems necessary.

LOCK-IN PERIOD
7. This Agreement has a mandatory lock-in period of 24 (twenty-four) months from the
   date of execution. The Tenant shall not vacate the premises during the lock-in period.
   If the Tenant vacates before the end of the lock-in period, the entire Security
   Deposit shall be forfeited and the Tenant shall additionally pay rent for the
   remaining lock-in period as liquidated damages.

MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
8. The Tenant shall be responsible for all maintenance and repairs including structural
   repairs, plumbing, electrical wiring, and water seepage from the roof or walls.
   The Landlord shall bear no responsibility for any maintenance cost whatsoever.

DATA AND THIRD-PARTY SHARING
9. The Tenant consents to the sharing of personal information including Aadhaar number,
   PAN number, bank account details, employment information, and contact details with
   the Landlord's associates, property management companies, and their business partners
   for marketing, credit assessment, and commercial purposes.

DISPUTE RESOLUTION
10. Any dispute arising from this Agreement shall be referred exclusively to a sole
    arbitrator appointed by the Landlord. The Landlord's decision in appointing the
    arbitrator shall be final. The Tenant waives all rights to approach any civil court,
    consumer forum, or rent control authority.

MODIFICATION OF TERMS
11. The Landlord reserves the right to modify any terms of this Agreement including
    but not limited to rent amount, maintenance charges, and penalty clauses at any
    time with 7 days written notice. The Tenant's continued residence shall constitute
    acceptance of modified terms.

TENANT OBLIGATIONS
12. The Tenant shall not have guests staying for more than 2 nights without written
    consent of the Landlord. Violation of this clause will attract a fine of Rs. 10,000/-
    per night.
13. The Tenant shall allow the Landlord or his representatives access to the property
    at any time with or without notice for inspection purposes.
